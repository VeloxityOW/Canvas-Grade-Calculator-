# Privacy Policy

Canvas Grade Calculator is designed to run entirely inside your browser.

## Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any user data.

## Grade Data

The extension reads grade information already displayed or loaded on your Canvas Grades page. This information is used only inside your browser to calculate an estimated grade.

No grade data is sent to any server.

## Login Information

This extension does not read, collect, or store usernames, passwords, login tokens, cookies, or authentication information.

## Third Parties

No data is shared with third parties.

## Contact

For questions or issues, open an issue on the GitHub repository.
